Spaghetti Recipe

Description

My familyâ€™s favorite weeknight friendly spaghetti recipe is quick and simple. The super flavorful spaghetti sauce is ready in 45 minutes and you can use your favorite ground meat to make it (beef, turkey, and chicken are all excellent).

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles as said on the instructions.
  2. Oil pan and set to medium high heat.
  3. Fry meatballs for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Pour in sauce and set to simmer.
  5. Serve with noodles and top with cheese while warm.
