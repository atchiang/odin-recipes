Pad Gra Pow (Thai Ground Beef)

Description

This is one of my favorite meals to make at home. It can be eated with just rice and a veggie of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 ounce of garlic
  • 1 ounce of ginger
  • 2.5 tablespoons of Soy Sauce
  • 2 ounces of Thai Basil

Steps

  1. Oil pan and set to medium high heat.
  2. Fry garlic and ginger for fragrance.
  3. Place ground beef in the pan and fry until almost brown.
  4. Pour soy sauce in and cook until sauce thickens to your liking.
  5. Serve warm on top of rice with a side of veggies.
