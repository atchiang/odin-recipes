Bibimbap Recipe

Description

An easy to make and extremely customizable dish!

Suggested Ingredients

  • Bulgogi(See other recipe on how to make it)
  • Pickled Radish
  • Bean sprout banchan
  • Sesame seeds
  • Blanched spinach

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients; there are many other things you can add, such as kimchi or seaweed sprinkles.
  2. Scoop some rice into a bowl, then line up ingredients in a circle inside your bowl.
  3. Take a picture, admire the dish, and then mix together to eat. Optionally, fry an egg or add gochujang sauce to also mix in.
